Doublethinker: Donald Trump

Doublethink is a term coined by George Orwell in his novel Nineteen Eighty-Four. It's used to describe one's ability to beleive in two opposing systems of thought simultaniously without seeing conflict in their positions. Today's examples of doublethink might include pro-lifers who, while beleiving in the sanctaty of life, also beleive in capital punishment and the execution of criminals. Another example might be the people who'll tell you that immigrants are lazy scroungers while also being the same people who steal our jobs.

Put best by George Orwell, doublethink is the ability

To tell deliberate lies while genuinely believing in them, to forget any fact that has become inconvenient, and then, when it becomes necessary again, to draw it back from oblivion for just so long as it is needed, to deny the existence of objective reality and all the while to take account of the reality which one denies

Today doublethink is the most dangerous tool of one of the globe's most powerful politicians. Donald trump uses it to misinform and shirk accountability. Unintentionally or not, he uses it to loft himself on a back and fourth platform immune from the inconvenient reality.
