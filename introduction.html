  • B: Benzoate

"...or you can call be B.O.B. Which ever's easeir."

B.O.B. is the end result of an experiment combining a genetically engineered tomato with chemically altered ranch salad dressing. When he was born, B.O.B. devoured six city blocks before he was captured by his long term mentor, General Monger in 1958. Following an arrest he was given a 'home' inside the prison complex of Area Fifty-Something.

"Did I come off a little too strong? I'm sorry, I'm a little rusty. I mean, I've been in prison my whole life."
